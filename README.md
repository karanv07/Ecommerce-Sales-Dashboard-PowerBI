# Ecommerce-Sales-Dashboard-PowerBI

## Project Overview
This project is an interactive Ecommerce Sales Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer purchase behavior, and business insights across different cities, states, categories, and payment methods.

The dashboard helps in tracking key metrics and identifying top-performing areas for better decision-making.

---

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales: 2M
- Total Profit: 37K
- Total Quantity: 6K
- Average Quantity: 3.74

---

## Dashboard Features
- KPI cards for Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Quantity, and Average Quantity
- Quarter-wise filtering
- City-level filtering
- Total sales by month analysis
- Top 5 cities by sales
- Top 5 states by sales
- Profit by category
- Profit by sub-category
- Sales by payment method

---

## Key Insights
- Indore and Mumbai are among the top-performing cities in terms of sales.
- Maharashtra generated the highest sales among all states shown in the dashboard.
- Clothing and Electronics contributed strongly to category-level profit.
- Printers and Bookcases were among the top-performing sub-categories.
- Cash on Delivery and EMI accounted for a significant share of payment methods.
